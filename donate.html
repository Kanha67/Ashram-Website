Frequently Asked Questions

Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes, Shiva Shakti Ashram is a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. All donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. You will receive a tax receipt for your records.

Can I cancel my monthly donation?

Yes, you can cancel your monthly donation at any time by contacting our office or through your account settings if you've created one.

How is my donation used?

Your donations support our spiritual programs, community outreach, maintenance of our facilities, and educational initiatives. We are committed to transparency and provide annual reports detailing how funds are allocated.

Can I donate in honor or memory of someone?

Yes, you can make a donation in honor or memory of a loved one. There is an option to include this information during the donation process, and we will send an acknowledgment to the person you designate.
