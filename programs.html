Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring to a program?

For most programs, we recommend bringing comfortable clothing for practice, a yoga mat, a meditation cushion, a water bottle, and any personal items you may need. Specific requirements will be provided in your program confirmation email.

Do I need prior experience to join your programs?

Our programs are designed for various levels of experience. While some programs like the Yoga Teacher Training require prior yoga experience, many of our workshops and retreats are suitable for beginners. Each program description indicates the recommended level of experience.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 30 days or more before the program start date are eligible for a full refund minus a 10% administrative fee. Cancellations made 14-29 days before the start date are eligible for a 50% refund. Cancellations made less than 14 days before the start date are not eligible for a refund.

Is accommodation provided for programs?

Accommodation is included in the program fee for retreats and longer programs. For workshops and shorter programs, accommodation can be arranged at an additional cost. Please contact us for details about accommodation options and pricing.

Are meals provided during programs?

Yes, vegetarian meals are provided for all residential programs. We accommodate dietary restrictions when notified in advance. For day programs, light refreshments are provided, and participants are welcome to bring their own lunch.
